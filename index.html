🌾 HAPPY Program

Participant Consent Form

Harnessing Agricultural Productivity and Prosperity for Youth (HAPPY) is committed to improving its programs and ensuring they have a positive impact on program participants. To achieve this, we collect and analyze data about program participants.

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This consent form explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information as part of the HAPPY Program. Please read this form carefully and sign to indicate your consent.

1. Purpose of Data Collection and Use

We collect your personal data to analyze the progress of the Program, improve the Program, and learn from the data collected, including monitoring and evaluating the effectiveness of the Harnessing Agricultural Productivity and Prosperity for Youth program and to learn how to improve it for future participants.

2. What Data Do We Collect?

We may collect some or all of the following information about you:

  • Name, age (date of birth), and gender
  • Contact details (contact number(s) and email)
  • Participation details in the Program
  • Level of Education
  • Marital status
  • Location or Residence, including region, district, and community
  • Refugee or displaced persons status
  • Presence of a disability
  • Feedback and responses to surveys
  • Audio-visual data including pictures, videos and voice recording

3. Who Will Your Data Be Shared With?

We may share your data with the following parties:

  • Agri-Impact Limited – the Program Management Unit
  • Funding Partner
  • Third-party data processors hired by Funding Partner to assist with monitoring and evaluation (e.g., data storage and analysis organizations)

For additional information on how personal data is processed, please contact +233 54 234 3168 to access a copy of the privacy policy. The Foundation's privacy policy can be accessed via https://mastercardfdn.org/privacy/

4. Your Rights

You are not obligated to give your consent. You are eligible to participate in all aspects of the program, regardless of your decision to share your data. Consent to data sharing is entirely separate from program participation. You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to erasure: Ask us to delete your information
  • Right to restriction of processing: Ask us to restrict processing of your information
  • Right to object to processing: Object to the processing of your information
  • Right to data portability: Request transfer of your data to another organization

5. Withdrawing Your Consent

You can withdraw your consent to share your data at any time. This will not affect your ability to participate in the program. To withdraw your consent, please contact: +233 54 234 3168

6. Understanding Your Choices

By consenting to share your data, you are helping ensure the success of the HAPPY program for you and future participants.
