Participant Consent Form

Harnessing Agricultural Productivity and Prosperity for Youth (HAPPY) is committed to improving its programs and ensuring they have a positive impact on participants. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Purpose of Data Collection and Use

We collect your personal data to analyze the progress of the Program, improve the Program, and learn from the data collected, including monitoring and evaluating the effectiveness of the Harnessing Agricultural Productivity and Prosperity for Youth program.

2. What Data Do We Collect?

We may collect some or all of the following information about you:

  • Name, age (date of birth), and gender
  • Contact details (phone number and email)
  • Participation details in the Program
  • Level of Education
  • Marital status
  • Location or Residence (region, district, community)
  • Refugee or displaced persons status
  • Presence of a disability
  • Feedback and responses to surveys
  • Audio-visual data (pictures, videos, voice recordings)

3. Who Will Your Data Be Shared With?

We may share your data with:

  • Agri-Impact Limited – the Program Management Unit
  • Funding Partner
  • Third-party data processors for monitoring and evaluation

For more information, contact +233 54 234 3168 or visit Privacy Policy

4. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal

  • Right to access: Request a copy of your information
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your information
  • Right to restriction: Restrict processing of your information
  • Right to object: Object to processing
  • Right to data portability: Transfer your data to another organization

5. Withdrawing Your Consent

You can withdraw your consent at any time by contacting: +233 54 234 3168. This will not affect your program participation.

6. Understanding Your Choices

By consenting, you help ensure the success of the HAPPY program for you and future participants. Participation in the program is not dependent on data sharing consent.
